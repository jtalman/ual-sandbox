ZLIB: Numerical Library for Differential Algebra

   
   
Description For many accelerator applications, Differential Algebra (DA) can be implemented on the space of high-order Truncated Power Series (TPS). ZLIB has been developed to provide the efficient numerical approach for fundamental operations with vectors of TPS and the relevant mapping techniques.
Author Yiton Yan
C++ Developers Nikolay Malitsky and Alexander Reshetov
References
